PURPOSE
Capture high-level governance, architecture, uniqueness, comparisons to other systems, and forward improvements.
This snapshot intentionally excludes Stuart/TG bug details.

WHAT SYNAPSE GOVERNANCE IS
- A governed AI operating system, not a UI or single agent.
- Canon-first execution: one authoritative working state.
- Evidence-gated completion: no success without machine-verifiable artifacts.
- Quest lifecycle: Accept → Execute → Verify → Audit → Complete.
- Authority separation: Human (“Hands”) decides; AI executes within constraints.
- Resumability: continuity locks + rehydration; no reliance on chat history.
- Interface-agnostic: Telegram/Web/CLI are adapters, not the core.

WHY IT IS UNIQUE
- Combines canon discipline, evidence-based truth, resumability, and audits as default law.
- Enforces honesty: the system cannot claim outcomes without proof.
- Designed and enforced operationally (not a doc-only framework).
- Built and validated end-to-end by a solo builder without institutional scaffolding.

COMPARISON TO OTHER SYSTEMS (PUBLIC)
- LangGraph: stronger primitives for checkpoints/interrupts; lacks an opinionated governance OS.
- SWE-agent / SWE-bench: stronger domain-specific proof (tests); narrow to SWE domain.
- OpenHands: stronger productization (UI/RBAC/sandboxing); different center of gravity.
- Eval tools (LangSmith/TruLens/Guardrails): stronger metrics; advisory, not authoritative.

WHAT TO ADOPT (PRIMITIVES ONLY)
- Checkpoint Artifacts (from LangGraph concept): hash-verified resume anchors.
- Domain-specific Truth Schemas (from SWE-agent): tests/files/devices as proof.
- Execution Profiles/Sandbox Adapters (from OpenHands): LOCAL_TRUSTED now; sandbox later.
- Evaluators as Artifacts (from eval tools): inform Truth Gate, never override it.

WHAT TO AVOID
- Graph DSLs replacing quests.
- UI-driven truth or metrics deciding completion.
- Multi-user/RBAC before governance is complete.

ROADMAP (GOVERNANCE-LED)
Phase 1: Wire Truth Gate universally (no success without evidence).
Phase 2: Add Checkpoint Artifacts + explicit resume anchors.
Phase 3: Execution Profiles (sandbox abstraction).
Phase 4: Optional evaluators as non-authoritative artifacts.

BOTTOM LINE
Synapse Governance is a coherent OS that can safely absorb primitives from others without losing integrity.
