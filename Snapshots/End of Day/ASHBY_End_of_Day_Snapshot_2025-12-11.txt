====================================================
2. CORE ARCHITECTURAL ACHIEVEMENTS
====================================================

--------------------------------
A. STATE INJECTION (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------

Implemented inject_state():

- Injects factual system-state updates directly into AshBrain history
- Uses system-role memory, not user-visible chat
- Allows Ashby to know what he just did

Example:
"I turned on thor lights to 60 percent."

This establishes:
ACTION → AWARENESS → CONTEXT CONTINUITY

--------------------------------
B. HALLUCINATION REJECTION (AGENCY LOCK)
--------------------------------

Implemented safe_chat() wrapper:

- All non-action chat routed through safe_chat
- Forbidden phrases blocked:
  - "I can't control"
  - "I don't have access"
  - "As an AI"
  - "I'm just"
  - Other agency-denial phrases

Final enforcement:
All handle_text fallbacks return safe_chat(), never brain.chat()

Result:
Ashby cannot deny his own agency or break immersion.

--------------------------------
C. ACTION → STATE ECHO (LIGHTING)
--------------------------------

Lighting actions now perform:

1. Physical action (Tuya API)
2. State injection describing result
3. Session continuation

Ashby knows:
- Which lights were changed
- To what level
- That HE performed the action
