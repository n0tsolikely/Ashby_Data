SYSTEM CONTEXT STATEMENT

Dungeon 10 chat must be a real assistant that answers questions using retrieved evidence, with citations, without hallucinating.

Canon 15 status:
- We have an internal LLM Gateway (ashby/interfaces/llm_gateway) used for formalizations (minutes/journal) only.

Missing for D10:
